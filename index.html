MAIN ACTIVITY

The main activity of the company is the import of grains and oilseeds, its further sales in the country, as well as exports to nearby markets.

Agricom customers are representatives of the mill sector, poultry plants, as well as all other small, medium and large companies in the food industry in Georgia and Armenia.

GRAIN TERMINAL

Poti Grain Terminal Llc is located in Poti, Georgia, along the berth N15 of the Poti Sea port. The main target of PGT is to service grain in-flow from and to Black Sea region.

PGT is the first alike terminal in Caucasus region with possibility to load grains from vessel by means of pneumatic machines and grain transporting gallery directly from/to grain silos.
